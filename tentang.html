About E-Modul

This web-based e-module is a modern learning tool designed with the Cognitive Theory of Multimedia Learning (CTML) principles. It helps students process information effectively, reduces unnecessary distractions, and strengthens understanding. The module includes animated videos that visually explain concepts and can be downloaded for offline learning. It also features interactive summaries that highlight key points, which are easy to follow and downloadable for flexible study. Additionally, students can test their knowledge through interactive quizzes with feedback to improve understanding.

The e-module is designed with a simple and minimalist layout, focusing on essential content to avoid distractions. Navigation is easy to use, making it accessible for students of all levels. The soft and comfortable color scheme ensures a pleasant learning experience. Moreover, this e-module works on all devices, including laptops, tablets, and smartphones, allowing students to learn anytime, anywhere. With its practical features and accessible design, this e-module supports effective, engaging, and flexible learning in the digital age.

About the Website's Target Students

The target students for this website are high school students at Sekola Indonesia Kuala Lumpur

Tentang Cara Menggunakan Website

1. Jika ingin mempelajari materi persamaan lingkaran, anda bisa membuka tab 'Materi'. Terdapat 3 sub materi yang dapat dipelajari di sana.
2. Jika ingin langsung mengerjakan latihan soal, maka anda bisa membuka tab 'Latihan Soal'. Terdapat 3 tipe latihan soal yang dapat dikerjakan di sana.
